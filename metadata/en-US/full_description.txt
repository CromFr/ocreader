OCReader is a feed reader for Nextcloud News (https://github.com/nextcloud/news), just login with your ownCloud/Nextcloud credentials and you can start reading your feeds.

It features a simple but colorful interface.


This app is free software released under the GPLv3+, the source code is available under https://github.com/schaal/ocreader

Issues and suggestions can be reported under https://github.com/schaal/ocreader/issues
